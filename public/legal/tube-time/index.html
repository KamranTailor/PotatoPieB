Tube Time Privacy Policy

Thank you for choosing Tube Time, the app that provides you with real-time train and bus schedules and status updates in London. This Privacy Policy is designed to help you understand how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

Tube Time collects only the necessary information to provide you with accurate and timely transportation information. We may collect:

  • Location Information: Tube Time uses your device's location services to determine the closest train stations and bus stops in London. However, we do not store your precise location or track your movements.
  • Usage Information: We may collect anonymous usage data, such as which features of the app you use most frequently, in order to improve our services.

2. How We Use Your Information

Tube Time uses the information we collect solely to enhance your experience and improve our services. Specifically, we may use your information to:

  • Determine your location to provide relevant train and bus schedules and status updates.
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve the functionality and performance of the app.
  • Communicate with you regarding updates, news, and important information related to Tube Time.

3. Information Sharing

We do not share, sell, or disclose your personal information to third parties except in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We may engage third-party service providers to assist us in providing and improving Tube Time. These service providers are contractually obligated to handle your information with confidentiality and only for the purposes of providing the services we require.
  • Legal Compliance: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid legal requests, such as court orders or subpoenas.

4. Data Security

Tube Time takes the security of your information seriously and employs industry-standard measures to protect it from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

Tube Time reserves the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
